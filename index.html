OblivionPath

OblivionPath is a dark fantasy indie game where every choice shapes your fate. Explore forgotten realms, face ancient horrors, and walk the path between light and oblivion.

Game World & Features

Enter a broken world filled with lost civilizations, corrupted creatures, and mysterious powers. OblivionPath focuses on atmosphere, exploration, and meaningful decisions.

Explore haunting environments filled with ancient ruins, twisted forests, and hidden secrets waiting to be uncovered.

Engage in intense combat where timing, positioning, and strategy matter. Every enemy encounter is a test of survival.

Discover the lore of a forgotten world through environmental storytelling, cryptic dialogues, and ancient artifacts.
