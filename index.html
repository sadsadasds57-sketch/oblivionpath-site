"Do I need to play Genesis Noir first?"

No. Both Genesis Noir and Depth Shade are written to be enjoyed and understood on their own. If you've played Genesis Noir the additional references and background on the characters might make for a richer experience of Depth Shade but, if not, your experience of Depth Shade won't be negatively impacted at all. You could even play them in reverse order - time is not a constant!

"What platforms can I play Depth Shade on?"

Depth Shade is currently being developed for Windows PC.

"Who is making Depth Shade?"

Depth Shade is being developed by KarenStudios, a small school studio in New York City. The music and sound design for Depth Shade is being created by Skillbards, a small team of composers and sound designers based in New York.
